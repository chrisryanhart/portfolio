About Me

“They told me I couldn’t. That’s why I did.” ~Michael Jordan. Born a skeptic, whenever I hear the words, “but that’s not possible!” I think to myself, “Is that really true?” For me, there are few things more satisfying then challenging norms to achieve the ‘impossible.’ It was with this mindset that I found a natural fit in the engineering world.

I graduated with a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering and went on to work in the auto industry. I started to see how profoundly beneficial software could be in problems I was facing day to day. Over time, I began incorporating more software skills into my toolbox, like Python. I saw the potential for more powerful, software-related solutions, but ultimately wasn’t empowered to pursue them at my work. That’s when I decided I wanted software to be an integral part of my career and left my job to study full-time.

In May of 2022, I started with Springboard in their Software Engineering Bootcamp. There, I focused on full stack web development where I mastered skills in JavaScript, AJAX, jQuery, Python, Flask, SQL, PostgreSQL, Node.js, Express.js, React.js, and Jest. I’ve already seen success by applying my software skills to various problems in my life and community, from fostering college debt mitigation to facilitating the meetup process.

Aside from coding, I’m an avid outdoor enthusiast who enjoys climbing and hiking. I also enjoy spending time time with my family and music (especially live).
